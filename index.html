Meeting with Jianyuan Wang

Joint PhD Student at Facebook AI Research and Visual Geometry Group (VGG), University of Oxford.

Jianyuan Wang is currently a joint PhD Student at Facebook AI Research and the Visual Geometry Group (VGG) at the University of Oxford. His research focuses on cutting-edge computer vision, collaborating with eminent academics David Novotny, Christian Rupprecht, and Andrea Vedaldi. Jianyuan is recognized for his work on VGGT: Visual Geometry Grounded Transformer, which received the Best Paper Award at CVPR 2025. VGGT represents a breakthrough, using a feed-forward transformer to perform fast, accurate 3D reconstruction from multiple views. It excels at estimating camera poses, reconstructing depth maps, and generating dense point clouds, all within seconds, without relying on traditional optimization routines.
